Introduction

Hello Gamers! This is Team Kingfisher, an up and coming International Gaming Organization with Clans, Tournaments and E-Sports, mainly focused on Supercell Games. We are an English speaking community, originated from India but house people from all parts of the world. We started as a small Clan in Clash Royale on 19th July 2020, which was just to seek some fun out of the game. Now, we are one of the most known Clan Families in Clash Royale with our Main Clan nearing #1k Global Clan Wars and Clans in multiple games running successfully along with regular Tournaments for Clash Royale. We have earned our place with the support of our Clanmates and the Community and help of our worthy Staff. We are grateful to everyone who has ever been a part of us. Do follow us on Twitter to stay updated.

Thank you,
