Introduction

Hello Gamers! This is Team Kingfisher, an ambitious growing Gaming Community with Clans, Tournaments and E-Sports for Supercell Games. We are an English speaking community, originated from India but house people from all parts of the world. We have been extending our limits since the day and we haven't stopped from entertaining our Gaming mates with new and exciting stuff. Kingfisher Gaming Hub is our solemn Discord community.
We started as a small Clan in Clash Royale on 19th July 2020, which was just to seek some fun out of the game. The wishes of our Community is our main aim. We have achieved it all with the help of our trust worthy Staff and Managers who aim to be the best. We are grateful to everyone who has ever been a part of the Community.
